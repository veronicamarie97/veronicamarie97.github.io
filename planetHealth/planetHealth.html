planetHealth

Brief

PlanetHealth is a supportive and active space dedicated to encouraging individuals to maintain their health and wellness. The gym offers a variety of training, a 24/7 hotline, and dozens of membership benefits.

Concept

I designed a brochure for marketing collateral with a simple color palette, and geometric and flat design styles in a neat layout to convey the most salient aspects of the gym.
